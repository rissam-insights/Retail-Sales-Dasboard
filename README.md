# 📊 Retail Sales Dashboard

## 🚀 Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover key insights related to revenue, profit, customer segments, and regional performance.  
An interactive dashboard was built using **Power BI** to enable data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Understand customer segment contribution  
- Evaluate regional sales distribution  
- Track monthly sales trends  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Microsoft Excel  
- Data Cleaning & Transformation  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- 💰 Total Sales: **29.83M**  
- 📈 Total Profit: **3.04M**  
- 🛒 Total Orders: **8399**  
- 🔻 Average Discount: **0.10**  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🛍️ Product Performance
- High-value products such as **Global Troy Executive Chair** and **Canon Copier** generated the highest revenue  
- Indicates strong demand in premium office and technology products  

---

### 🏷️ Category Analysis
- **Technology** contributes the highest share (~40%) of total sales  
- Followed by **Furniture** and **Office Supplies**  
- Technology products are the primary revenue drivers  

---

### 👥 Customer Segment Analysis
- **Corporate segment** generates the highest profit contribution  
- Home Office and Small Business segments show moderate performance  
- Suggests strong B2B sales potential  

---

### 🌍 Regional Performance
- Top-performing regions include:
  - West  
  - Ontario  
  - Prairie  
- Lower performance observed in Nunavut and Northwest regions  
- Opportunity for regional expansion strategies  

---

### 📅 Monthly Trends
- Sales fluctuate across months with noticeable peaks around **May and September**  
- Indicates possible seasonal demand trends
